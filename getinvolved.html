Get Involved

American Red Cross

In partnership with the Georgia Tech Blood Drive committee, the American Red Cross Club at Georgia Tech strives to hold successful blood drives and make an impact on local and nationwide communities. Through blood donations, Georgia Tech is helping people across the country who are suffering from injuries or illnesses. If you are interested in joining a Red Cross Committe, please email gtredcross@gmail.com with a description of your interest.

Providing Opportunities

With the American Red Cross Club at Georgia Tech, students can expect opportunities to obtain quality leadership experience working on organizing, planning, and providing services for Georgia Tech and Atlanta. Our goal is to make a difference by hosting blood drives and participating in community service activities throughout the year. The One Day Blood Drive is a national blood drive campaign completely organized by Georgia Tech students to commemorate those who lost their lives during 9/11 and other national disasters. You can find more information at the One Day Project page.
