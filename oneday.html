One Day Project

National Blood Drive Campaign

The One Day Blood Drive Project is a national event completely organized and planned by the American Red Cross Club and the Blood Drive Committee at Georgia Tech. The blood drive is to take place in all 50 states with over 70 schools supporting our cause. Our goal is to commemorate those men and woman who had lost their lives during 9/11 and other tragic events. On September 11, 2014 the blood drive is to take place and we strive to make this a successful event for years to come. You can find more information and a listing of the schools participating at our dedicated site for the One Day Project.
